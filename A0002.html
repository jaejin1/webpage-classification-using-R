Full details of these interest rates and those applicable to accounts no longer available can be found in a schedule permanently available on the counter at all branches and agents of Alliance & Leicester plc. The above rates are variable. Gross rates of interest do not take account of deduction of income tax. Except for ISA´s and TESSAs, interest will be payable net of income tax at the rate determined by Inland Revenue, from time to time, or, subject to the required registration, gross. Registration forms are available at your nearest Alliance & Leicester branch. Tax may be reclaimed from the Inland Revenue where the amount deducted exceeds a saver's liability to tax. Similarly, if you are liable to higher rate tax, additional income tax may be payable to the Inland Revenue. Interest rates are subject to variation. Where gross monthly rates are shown they are gross Annual Equivalent Rates. Interest is calculated daily from the day following receipt, up to and including the day before withdrawal, except for investments made by cheque where interest is calculated from the fourth banking day following receipt (Saturdays, Sundays & Bank Holidays are not banking days).

* AER stands for Annual Equivalent Rate and illustrates what the interest rate would be if interest was paid and compounded each year. The AER is calculated on the Gross rate without the bonus. As every advert for an Interest bearing product will contain an AER, you will be able to compare more easily what return you can expect over time.

Holders of closed branch based or postal accounts can transfer their balances to an open account from our current savings range without charge (subject to the open account terms and conditions). Closed account holders can contact either a Customer Advisor at your nearest branch or a Customer Services Advisor at the Postal Savings Centre.
